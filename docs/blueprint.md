## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vibrant Purple (#A020F0) to reflect the grandeur and artistry of Queen's music combined with a symphonic orchestra.
- Background color: Dark Grey (#333333) for a sophisticated, concert hall atmosphere.
- Accent color: Electric Blue (#7DF9FF) to highlight important elements and call to action buttons, giving a modern and energetic feel.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif suitable for body text
- Headline font: 'Playfair', a modern sans-serif suitable for headlines
- Use icons inspired by Queen's most iconic imagery such as crowns and guitars.
- Subtle animations and transitions that provide a visually appealing experience without being distracting.
